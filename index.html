Cascade Messages Effectively

Leaders are responsible for translating market-unit messages into something their teams can hear, understand, and act on. Cascading well means consistent timing, consistent content, and a chance for two-way dialogue.

Listening Transforms Leadership

Listening sessions create space for teammates to share what's working, what isn't, and what they need from leadership. The themes we hear directly shape our People Plan priorities throughout the year.

What This Means for Leaders

Show up to listening sessions ready to truly listen, cascade messages on a consistent cadence, and close the loop with your teams on what you heard and what's changing. Communication is a daily leadership habit, not a quarterly event.
